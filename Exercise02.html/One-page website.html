HEREDITARY

Plot

After her mother passes away, Annie and the rest of the family are grief-stricken. Soon, strange things start occurring and the horrifying truth about Annie's ancestry begins to come to light.

Key Information

  • Title: hereditary
  • Director: Ari Aster
  • Writer: Ari Aster
  • Release Date: June 08,2018
  • Genre: Horror,psycological Thriller
  • Language: English
  • Box Office: $8.8 million

Characters

  • Toni Collette (Juror #2) as Annie Graham, an artist
  • Gabriel Byrne (Maniac) as Steve Graham, Annie’s husband
  • Alex Wolff (Oppenheimer) as Peter Graham, Annie and Steve’s son
  • Milly Shapiro (Hysteria!) as Charlie Graham, Annie and Steve’s daughter
  • Ann Dowd (The Handmaid’s Tale) as Joan, who attends group therapy with Annie
  • Mallory Bechtel (Pretty Little Liars: Original Sin)as Bridget, Peter’s classmate

Creative-Team

  • Producers: Kevin Frakes, Lars Knudsen, Buddy Patrick
  • Cinematography: Pawel Pogorzelski
  • Music: Colin stetson
